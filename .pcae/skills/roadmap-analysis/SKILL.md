## Purpose

Inspect PCAE roadmap position, successor phases, and drift risks without mutating roadmap state.

## Workflow

1. Read the roadmap registry and current phase.
2. Compare active phase, predecessor, successor, and track alignment.
3. Surface completed, superseded, and gap phases relevant to the request.
